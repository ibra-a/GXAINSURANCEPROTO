## üí° Key Speaking Tips

### Do's ‚úÖ

1. **Lead with value, not price**
   - Always show what it saves before saying what it costs

2. **Use numbers confidently**
   - "70% faster" not "much faster"
   - "$35,000-80,000 savings" not "significant savings"

3. **Pause after big numbers**
   - Let "$15,000" sink in
   - Let "$35,000-80,000 savings" sink in

4. **Show, don't just tell**
   - Demo first, price second
   - Let them see value before cost

5. **Be confident**
   - You built something great
   - Price is fair
   - You've earned it

6. **Listen to concerns**
   - Don't rush to defend
   - Understand their perspective
   - Then respond thoughtfully

### Don'ts ‚ùå

1. **Don't apologize for price**
   - It's fair and justified
   - You've earned it

2. **Don't start with low price**
   - Start confident
   - Can negotiate if needed

3. **Don't rush**
   - Take your time
   - Especially during demo
   - Especially during pricing discussion

4. **Don't oversell**
   - Let demo speak for itself
   - Be honest about capabilities

5. **Don't get defensive**
   - Stay professional
   - Address concerns calmly
